"Behavioral neuroscience" seems to capture what I do the best. The inaccurate part is - I have always felt the phrase "behavioral neuroscience" emphasizes neuroscience more than behavior, whereas I want to study behavior and am just studying it from a neuroscientific perspective. Ultimately, my goal is to apply scientific knowledge about the brain and the body to impact behavior, i.e. improving athletic performance, especially in women's basketball.
